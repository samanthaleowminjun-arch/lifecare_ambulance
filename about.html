About Us

Life Care Services and Event Private Limited was incorporated in 2002, building upon a healthcare foundation that began under Lifecare Ambulance and Services, a sole proprietorship established by a dedicated Staff Nurse.

From modest we have grown into a trusted provider of compassionate discharge services, medical transport solutions, and event medical coverage across Singapore and beyond.

Our Core Services: Medical Transport & Compassionate Discharge

Our primary line of service is pre-scheduled medical ferry and patient transport, providing safe and reliable transfers between nursing homes, hospitals, specialist clinics, and residences.

We work closely with:

St Andrew’s Nursing Home (Henderson, Queenstown, Taman Jurong; National University Hospital (NUH); Singapore General Hospital (SGH); Diabetic & Metabolism Centre and Eye Clinic; IMH; KKH Emergency Department

Our services include:

Two-way scheduled hospital appointments with medical escort services by trained Life Care staff & ad-hoc one-way transfers (including Emergency Departments)

Cross-border medical transport to Johor Bahru, Kuala Lumpur, and George Town (Penang) with Oxygen-supported land transfers; Transfers to ferry terminals for Batam via fast boat; Airport medical transfers with FAA-certified portable oxygen concentrators; Commercial airline medical travel (with tarmac access); Private jet transfers for critically ill patients.

End-of-Life Support & eRBD Certification

24-Hour Doctor-to-Home End-of-Life Certification (eRBD)

Our on-call medical doctors attend to patients at their residence for end-of-life certification and electronic Registration of Births & Deaths (eRBD) procedures.

Our experience includes:

Standard Chartered Marathon (in collaboration with SGH); Open sea triathlons; Handicap swimming events; Singapore Sports Council U-17 soccer training across four stadiums (24-month programme with live tele medical supervision); 24-hour standby coverage at Waterloo Street Guan Yin Temple during Chinese New Year (Eve, Day 1, Day 2, and 26th Day of Lunar New Year); COVID-19 health screening services since 2019

Our teams are trained to handle injuries, medical emergencies, and on-site treatments with efficiency and professionalism.
